About Teiid

Teiid offers a relational abstraction of all information sources that is highly performant and allows for integration with your existing relational tools. Teiid has an accompanying easy-to-use design tool that enables data architects to integrate disparate information in minutes. 

Teiid has a long history of addressing the data access needs of any size enterprise environment.  Have a look at Why Teiid?, our feature matrix, and FAQ to see if this project is a good fit for your needs.

Best of all, Teiid is open source and a part of the JBoss Community of projects.
